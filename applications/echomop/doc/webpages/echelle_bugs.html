EDR Bug Reports

Before you report a bug you might want to check that it has not been reported before.

Previous bug reports and their status are available.

Use the form provided to report bugs you have found. Please be as complete in your description of the bug as possible.

Remember that your comments will be publicly available for anyone to read. If you don't want your opinions generally aired, send your reports to mjc@star.ucl.ac.uk instead.

Bug reports should also be made to ussc@starlink.rutherford.ac.uk.
