Hints

ECHOMOP provides four extraction methods:

  • ECH_QEXTR does a quick look extraction.
  • ECH_EXTRCT EXTRACT_MODE=S does a simple summed extraction.
  • ECH_EXTRCT EXTRACT_MODE=P does profile weighted extraction.
  • ECH_EXTRCT EXTRACT_MODE=O does `optimal' weighted extraction.
