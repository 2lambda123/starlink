MAX_DISPERSION

Usage

MAX_DISPERSION=_REAL

Description

The range of dispersions to be searched by the automatic line identifier is specified in terms of minimum and maximum Wavelength Unit per pixel. Care should be taken to ensure that the specified range is wide enough. If in doubt use a larger range than is probably needed. For example, if the actual dispersion is expected to be approx .5, then a range of .2 to .8 would not be inappropriate, whereas a range of .4 to .6 could possibly be too restrictive. The normal wavelength units will be Angstroms, but alternative units may be used by providing an appropriate .ARC line list.
