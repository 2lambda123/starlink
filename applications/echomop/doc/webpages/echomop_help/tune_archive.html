TUNE_ARCHIVE

Usage

TUNE_ARCHIVE=_LOGICAL

Description

Set to YES if automatic archiving of results files is to be performed. This is the default setting and users are encouraged to leave archiving enabled whilst they are reducing real data frames. The archive is maintained by a central site and will support query facilities. A 2 year data protection scheme is applied, so your spectra may not be accessed by other parties until 2 years after its inclusion in the archive. See HELP ECHOMOP ARCHIVING for a more detailed discussion.

The default value is YES.
