Auto

A(uto), initiates an automatic search and match of the reference feature database. Any preset limits on the wavelength and dispersion search range are taken into account. Each possible solution is reported upon with an indication of its probable accuracy. The fit may then be to rejected, or examined in detail for verification or modification.
