ECHOMOP News

Martin Clayton, Starlink. 8th December 1995.

This HELP facility accompanies ECHOMOP V3.1-0.

This is an interim release of ECHOMOP with some bug fixes.

Digital UNIX users will notice a significant performance improvement with this release.

ECHOMOP is now being fully supported by Starlink. You are encouraged to make suggestions for improvements and report bugs. Bug fixes will be made available in releases throughout 1996 as they become available.

Please report bugs to starlink@jiscmail.ac.uk and to mjc@star.ucl.ac.uk.
