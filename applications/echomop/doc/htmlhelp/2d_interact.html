2D_INTERACT

Usage

2D_INTERACT=_LOGICAL

Description

Set TRUE for interactive control of 2-D polynomial fitting of distortion corrections.

Polynomials are fitted in two perpendicular directions (rows and columns) and generate the point-to-point predicted deviation from wavelength fit for each pixel centre. Interactive operation allows viewing of an exagerrated version of the fitted points, and selective clipping of points on the basis of their deviations from the fit.
