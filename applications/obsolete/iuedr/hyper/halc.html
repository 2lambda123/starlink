HALC

Usage

HALC=number

Description

This is the Halation correction constant used for HALTYPE =POWER cases, and defined at wavelength HALWC . The value of the correction constant corresponds, roughly to the measured depression of a broad zero intensity absorption below "zero", in units of the continuum in adjacent orders. The "constant" varies with wavelength: 

      C(WAV) = HALC * (WAV - HALW0) / (HALWC - HALW0)

See the HALAV , HALTYPE , HALW0 and HALWC parameters.
